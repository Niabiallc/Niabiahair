Care

NIA BIA Hair is curated for longevity, reinstallation, and consistent performance over time. Care is treated as maintenance, not trend.

Standard

Washing

Cleanse as needed based on wear and buildup. Use lukewarm water and a gentle shampoo. Condition through mid-lengths and ends. Rinse thoroughly.

Standard

Drying

Blot with a towel—do not rub. Air dry when possible. If blow-drying, use controlled temperature and continuous movement.

Standard

Heat + Styling

Heat is permitted. Always use a heat protectant and moderate temperature. Avoid repeated high-heat passes on the same section.

Standard

Storage

Store hair fully dry. Keep bundles in a satin bag or breathable pouch. Avoid compression, humidity, and friction.
