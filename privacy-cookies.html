NIA BIA Privacy & Cookies Policy

NIA BIA respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy & Cookies Policy explains how information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you visit or make a purchase from our website.

Information We Collect

When you interact with NIA BIA, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, shipping and billing details, and payment information. We may also collect limited technical data, including IP address, browser type, and device information, to improve site performance and security.

How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used solely to process orders, provide client support, communicate updates, and improve our services. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, analyze site traffic, and support essential site functions. Cookies may remember your preferences and help us understand how clients interact with our site. You may disable cookies through your browser settings; however, some features of the site may not function properly.

Data Protection & Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. Payment information is processed securely through trusted third-party providers and is not stored on our servers.

Third-Party Services

NIA BIA may use third-party service providers to operate our website, process payments, or deliver communications. These providers are granted access only to the information necessary to perform their services and are required to maintain confidentiality.

Your Privacy Rights

In accordance with applicable U.S. and Florida privacy laws, you have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, subject to certain limitations. Requests may be submitted through our Contact page.

Policy Updates

NIA BIA reserves the right to update this Privacy & Cookies Policy at any time. Changes will be reflected on this page and are effective immediately upon posting.

By using our website, you consent to the terms outlined in this policy.
