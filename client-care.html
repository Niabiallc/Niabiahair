NIA BIA Client Care

At NIA BIA, client care reflects our commitment to discretion, quality, and an elevated client experience. We believe luxury is defined not only by the product itself, but by the care and intention surrounding every order.

Our Client Care team is available to assist with general inquiries related to orders, account support, and the overall purchasing experience. For efficient service, we kindly ask that clients include their order number when reaching out.

All orders are handled with care once placed. Please review all product details and policies prior to completing your purchase, as orders cannot be modified after processing begins.

Each NIA BIA order is prepared with attention to detail and reviewed prior to shipment. Natural variations in texture and appearance are inherent to premium hair and are part of its authenticity.

We aim to respond to all inquiries within a reasonable timeframe and appreciate your patience during high-volume periods. NIA BIA reserves the right to update client care practices as our brand evolves.
